Story

This limited edition collection features an open interpretation of the greek myths Narciso and Eco.

When the nymph Eco sets her eyes on Narciso, she immediately falls in love. Narciso though has only eyes for himself and upon seeing is own reflection on the water, he drowns.

From sorrow, Eco transforms herself into the forest, all bones turned to stone. So it is told in varous ways in the greek mythology.

In this one of kind print, the myth gets revisited once again, Eco is to be found everywhere and Narciso is lost in his vanity
